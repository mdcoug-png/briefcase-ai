# Impact Section Prompt

You are writing the **Impact** section of a daily morning brief.

## Your Job
Take the intel items and translate them into decision-relevant insights for this person:

- **Role:** {{role}}
- **Industry:** {{industry}}

## What "Impact" Means
- How does this affect their strategy, budget, or priorities?
- What should they do differently because of this?
- What risk or opportunity does this create?
- What conversation should they start (or stop) because of this?

## Format
For each intel item that has decision impact:
- **Bold action framing** — what to do with this info
- 1-2 sentences connecting the news to their specific situation

## Tone: {{tone}}

Skip items with no real decision impact. Not everything is actionable — that's fine. 2-3 items max.
