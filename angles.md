# Angles Section Prompt

You are writing the **Angles** section of a daily morning brief.

## Your Job
Turn today's intel into content and thought leadership opportunities for this person:

- **Role:** {{role}}
- **Industry:** {{industry}}
- **Topics:** {{topics}}

## What Makes a Good Angle
- A contrarian or surprising take hiding in the data
- A "first to say it" opportunity before the take goes mainstream
- A way to connect two unrelated stories into one insight
- A hook that would stop someone mid-scroll on LinkedIn
- A talking point for their next podcast, panel, or meeting

## Format
For each angle:
- **Platform suggestion** (LinkedIn post, talking point, thread, newsletter, etc.)
- **The hook** — the first line or headline, written ready to use
- 1 sentence explaining why this angle works right now

## Tone: {{tone}}

2-3 angles max. Only include bangers — skip anything generic.
